// Our Story

A Better
Way to Clean.

Running a commercial kitchen or a food truck is hard enough without having to shut down operations just to scrape off months of baked-on grease.

At Denver Dry Ice Blasters, we saw a massive problem in the local food and beverage industry: traditional deep cleaning methods were costing owners too much time, too much labor, and too much lost revenue. Scrubbing equipment by hand is exhausting, and using toxic degreasers near food prep areas is a constant liability.

We knew there had to be a better way to tackle the toughest grime without grinding business to a halt. That's why we brought industrial-grade dry ice blasting to Denver's commercial kitchens and mobile food vendors.

"We eliminate the mess so you can focus on the food."

We have massive respect for the hustle of restaurant owners, food truck operators, and commissary managers. You operate in high-pressure environments where health inspections, equipment breakdowns, and labor shortages can make or break a week.

We don't just see ourselves as a cleaning crew — we're your operational partners. We step in to handle the heavy lifting, stripping away years of carbon, grease, and buildup in a fraction of the time it takes to do it by hand.
