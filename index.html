// Why Dry Ice

The Ultimate
Power Move.

A high-pressure blast of food-grade CO₂ that vaporizes on impact. Zero residue. Zero damage. Maximum lift.

01 / Speed

Zero Teardown.

We clean your heavy-duty equipment exactly where it sits. No taking fryers, hoods, or mixers apart, which means no lost prep time.

02 / Safety

100% Food-Safe.

Food-grade dry ice blasts grime away. Leaves zero toxic residue. Fully approved by the FDA, EPA, and USDA.

03 / Process

No Water,
No Mess.

The dry ice sublimates instantly — no wastewater to mop, completely safe around electrical outlets and motorized equipment.

04 / Result

Tough on Grease.
Soft on Steel.

Strips the most stubborn baked-on carbon without scratching stainless, plastics, gaskets, or finishes.

// Operations Log

A Single
Service Day.

Most kitchens are back online before dinner service. Here's how a typical job runs from arrival to handoff.

07:30 MST
Step 01 · Arrival

Mobile blast rig pulls up to your back-of-house.

We work on your schedule — early morning, between services, or full overnight blocks.

07:45
Step 02 · Walkthrough

5-minute equipment audit and prep.

Sensitive components masked, prep surfaces protected, exhaust opened up for airflow.

08:00
Step 03 · The Blast

Dry ice pellets hit at −109°F.

Carbon, grease and bio-film flash-freeze and lift off in seconds. The dry ice vaporizes mid-air. Nothing left behind but the gunk.

10:30
Step 04 · Sweep

Hardened debris swept up — no wastewater.

What was years of build-up is now a pile on the floor. Clean it like an evening's trash.

11:00
Step 05 · Handoff

Walkthrough, photos, signed report.

Health-inspection-ready documentation handed to your manager. You're back online for lunch.
